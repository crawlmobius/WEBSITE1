Bay of Plenty

Welcome to Bay of Plenty

New Zealand is located in the South Pacific Ocean, approx 2000km South-East of Australia, is made up of three main islands - the North Island, South Island & Stewart Island.

The Bay of Plenty is a region of New Zealand on the east coast of the North Island of New Zealand. This region takes in the full sweep of the coastline from Lottin Point in the east, to Waihi Beach in the west. Take a look through our website and let us show you how to discover the Bay of Plenty in all of its splendour.

The Bay of Plenty lies east of the Kaimai-Mamaku Ranges and south of the Coromandel peninsula. It is the perfect place for a family holiday with it's beautiful harbours, long white surf beaches, friendly people and an easy going lifestyle. With some excellent surf spots, sun, sand, sea and a myriad of great places to eat and shop there will be no shortage of fun for all the family.

Located at the entrance to a beautiful natural harbour is Tauranga--a city with a nautical theme. With many bars, clubs and restaurants Tauranga is great for a quite meal or a night out with friends. Tauranga also has many great shops and shopping centres for you to check out during the day and is also very close to the mount.



How to get here

  • Scenic drive by private car from Auckland via State Highway 27 - 3 hrs
  • Scenic drive by public bus - 4 hrs
  • Scenic drive from Rotorua (private vehicle) - 1 hr
  • Scenic drive from Rotorua (public bus) - 1,5 hr
  • By plane from Wellington to Tauranga - 1 hr 15 min
  • Scenic drive from Wellington (public bus) - 10 hr
