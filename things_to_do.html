Bay of Plenty: Things to Do

Things to Do and See

Kiwi 360
At Kiwi360,Te Puke, there is a celebration of the incredible NZ Kiwifruit. New Zealand is a the Kiwifruit Capital of the world!
Dolphin Seafaris
Dolphin Seafaris will take you on a marine adventure to watch or swim with wild dolphins in their natural habitat. 4-5 hour trips departing daily from Tauranga and Mount Maunganui in our stable and comfortable high speed launch.
NATIVConnectioNZ
This is the chance to learn how to prepare, lay and eat an authentic earth cooked Maori hangi. Whakatane
Waimarino Wairoa River Tour
Explore the upper reaches of the Wairoa River from Omanawa,Tauranga. No prior experience required for this picturesque flat water paddle to Waimarino.
Flatout Riding Classic Motorcycle Tours
Experience New Zealand's stunning countryside from the seat of a classic 1975 Norton Commando MKIII. Whakatane
Adrenalin Forest
Affordable fun for the whole family, a wicked adrenalin rush adventure park in a beautiful forest setting in TECT All Terrain Park 20minutes from Rotorua and Tauranga. Over 25 flying foxes and 100 different challenges to overcome.
NZ Kayaker Kayak Fishing Tours
With a life time of experience in kayaks, we specialise in providing unique tours and excellent product service all based around the wonderfull sport that is kayaking. Tauranga
Mt Maunganui (Mauao) Summit Walk
Venture to the top of our most famous landmark. This walk takes about 40 minutes and requires a good level of fitness. Choose from two scenic tracks to reach the 232m-high summit. The view at the top is worth the effort.
