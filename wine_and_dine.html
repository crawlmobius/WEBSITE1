Bay of Plenty: Wine and Dine

Wine and Dine

Tauranga: Satori Lounge
At Satori Lounge our concept is sushi rolled fresh to order just for you. Our warm deep fried rolls are perfect for converting non sushi lovers and most of our menu can be made gluten free.
Tauranga: The Dry Dock Cafe
Gorgeous cafe on Wharf St. Great coffee, great food and great service.
Tauranga: Little India
Using recipes from the kitchen of our Grandmother Premjit Kaur Gill. Our menus feature authentic and delicious dishes from North India, especially the Punjab.
Tauranga: Elizabeth Cafe and Larder
From signature dishes such as the crispy snapper taco and eggs Benedict on a potato and herb rosti, to succulent pastries that are made fresh on site every day, there is plenty to tempt all.
Tauranga: O Factorie - Tasting Shed
We are a new breed of cider makers twisting traditional methods to create modern day ciders that are refreshing, full of flavor and produced locally.
Whakatane: L'Epicerie, French deli and cafe
Come and try our authentic French food, source you special ingredients and enjoy a cup of Allpress coffee. L'Epicerie is a special place mixing delicatessen and cafe to double the pleasure.
Katikati: Katikati Bird Gardens
A beautiful and extensive park and bird sanctuary on the edge of the inner Tauranga harbour; the bird gardens feature a wide range of native and colourful birds.
Papamoa: Rain Bar
Located in the seaside town of Papamoa, Rain Bar is the ideal spot to enjoy a delicious meal or a night out with friends. A vibrant woodfired pizza restaurant serving quality food at affordable price.
