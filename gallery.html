Today's video shows beatiful walk to the summit of Mount Maunganui.

Mauao is a historic reserve owned by Maori and managed by Tauranga City Council. The base track is 3.4 km and takes about 45 minutes to walk. It is suitable for strollers and wheelchairs but please take care. There are several tracks to the summit. The two tracks recommended for sightseers are the Oruahine and Waikorire. The summit is 232m high – it takes about 40 minutes to get to the top.

There's no climbing involved, but the tracks and inclines are steep, with stairs and loose gravel. This strenuous (2 hour) return hike will reward with unforgettable 360° panoramic views of Bay of Plenty
